Introducing‘ ”AHOLE” - a revolutionary interpretation of a square yet circular typeface. Typically, the term “A-hole” is employed as an expletive. However, in this context, it assumes a dual significance. An oval shape, as a counter, is incorporated into the font’s design, giving it a touch of creativity. This typeface presents a harmonious piece of art, reminiscent of an oval.
